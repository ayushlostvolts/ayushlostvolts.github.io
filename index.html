About LostVolts

Ayush Krishna Nandy, also known by his stage name LostVolts, is an Indian Music Producer whose emotional, melodic & energetic music has made quite an impact on the new-age revival of Progressive House.

He fell in love with Electronic Music when he was 15 and has been constantly improving his sounds and skills. He is heavily inspired by none other than HARDWELL. LostVolts started his career by winning the first-ever REVEALED RECORDINGS x IZOTOPE Producer's Contest and has released various tracks on Revealed Recordings, Smash The House, Glow Records, FeelQ Recordings, etc.

LostVolts has been supported by his family and by some of the biggest names in the industry, including Hardwell, Revealed Recordings, Tomorrowland, NAEMS, Jay Hardway, Timmy Trumpet, Nicky Romero, Dastic, Jonas Aden, Mike Williams, Castor & Pollux, etc.
